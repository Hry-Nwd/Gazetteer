About Me

I have spent the past few years travelling the world on some of the largest private superyachts, teaching their owners water sports and learning some great skills along the way. I am currently training to be a web developer via online training I am looking for a role in which I can break into the IT industry with a reputable company. I thrive when working in a team, but also enjoy using my initiative to problem solve independently when required. I am always keen to expand my knowledge base and I am constantly looking for ways to go above and beyond what is expected of me.
